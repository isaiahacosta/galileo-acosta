WELCOME TO NEWSROOM


"At Manolo Fortich National High School, the partnership between the SPTA (School Parents-Teachers Association) and SOSA (Student Organizations and Societies Association) plays a vital role in fostering a strong sense of community and collaboration. Through their collective efforts, they are creating an environment where students, parents, and teachers work hand in hand to enhance academic success, promote leadership, and ensure the holistic development of every student. Together, SPTA and SOSA are laying the foundation for a brighter, more inclusive future for the entire school community."



"The Mobile Legends Tournament at Manolo Fortich National High School was an exciting showcase of teamwork, strategy, and skill. Students from various year levels came together to compete in an electrifying atmosphere, demonstrating not only their gaming abilities but also their passion for friendly competition and camaraderie. This event not only entertained but also fostered school spirit and unity, highlighting the importance of collaboration and sportsmanship in every game. A true testament to the growing e-sports culture at the school!"



"The Ms. and Mr. Palaro at Manolo Fortich National High School was a celebration of talent, charisma, and school pride. This annual event brought together students from different year levels to showcase their diverse skills, from pageantry to athleticism. Contestants not only displayed their grace and confidence on stage, but also demonstrated their commitment to excellence in both academics and extracurriculars. The event highlighted the beauty of diversity, unity, and the strong spirit of competition that thrives within the school community."



"The tree planting activity at Manolo Fortich National High School was a meaningful step towards environmental stewardship and sustainability. Students, teachers, and staff united to plant trees on the school grounds, symbolizing their commitment to preserving nature for future generations. This event not only contributed to the beautification of the campus but also fostered a sense of responsibility among the students to care for the environment. Through this simple yet impactful initiative, the school community is taking an active role in creating a greener, healthier world."
